Soy vs Paraffin

Soy Wax

Soy wax is made from soybean oil. It can contain other non soy materials such as other animal or plant based products. Soy wax is better for the environment and is a renewable resource.

Buy Now

Paraffin Wax

Paraffin wax is made out of petroleum made from crude oil. Crude oil is a fossil fuel and is not renewable.

Scent

Soy wax is more dense than paraffin wax meaning it has to burn hotter to release the fragrance. This can lead to paraffin wax releasing scent faster

Burn time

Since soy wax is denser and requires more heat to burn it can have a longer burn time than paraffin wax.

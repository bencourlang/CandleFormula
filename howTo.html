How to Make Candles

  • First make a double boiler system with a pot below with boiling water. Put a pot on top of the pot below for the wax to be melted in.
  • Measure out how much wax you will be putting into the pot.
    • This can be done either by cups and then calculating the corresponding weight or using a scale.
  • You will then put the wax into the pot/bowl and melt it with your double boiler system
  • While the wax is melting, prepare your jars.
    • Make sure the inside of the jar is clean
    • Place your wick in the bottom of the jar with a wick sticker or glue.
  • When wax is melted add your desired colors to the wax in concentrations corresponding with the color that you have. Mix in thoroughly
  • Let the wax cool to about 120 degrees (this may vary based on the wax you are using) before adding the fragrance then mix thoroughly. You want to add
  • When mixed, pour your wax into your jars.
  • When poured secure the wick with a wick bar
    • You can make your own by putting a hole the wick fits through in a small piece of cardboard.
  • Let the candles cool. Do not put then in a water bath or the fridge to speed up the cooling
